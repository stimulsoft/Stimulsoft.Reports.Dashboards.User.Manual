## Band Types

There are many bands in Stimulsoft Reports, each with its own unique capabilities. All bands can be categorized into one of two types: standard bands and cross bands.


**Standard Bands**

Standard bands are rendered from top to bottom. They are typically placed directly on a page or can be placed on a panel.


**Cross Bands**

Cross bands are rendered from left to right. They are usually placed on standard bands. There is one special category of band called the Child band, which, although it is a standard band, is commonly used to extend a Data band.
